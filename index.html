Mrs. Leslie Manly

Hi!  My name is Leslie Manly.  I am so very excited to be your child's Kindergarten teacher this year.  We are going to have an awesome year!  This year will begin my 10th year of teaching and my 3rd year at Onalaska Elementary and Onalaska ISD.     When I am not at school, I really enjoy spending with my sons, Matthew (He starts 2nd grade this year!) and Luke (He will be 3 in November!), my daughter, Emily (She's in 6th grade!), my husband and our family and friends. I also LOVE to read, be outside, do crafts, bake and of course go shopping!  As the year goes on you will learn a lot more about me and I can not wait to learn about you and your family.
